Our Services

Main Services

Our main services are as follows-

Zane tech International is a specialized trading, indenting & supplying firm in Bangladesh. It is also an exclusive representative of internationally reputed manufacturers and suppliers to supply Machines, Tools, Raw-materials and accessories to different types of government & non-government organization in Bangladesh by participating tender.

During Tender Process

  • Pursuing technical offer with procurement authority.
  • Pursuing procurement proposal and signing the contract between customer and foreign manufacture/Principle.
  • Arrange PG.
  • Execution and opening LC.
  • Coordinate for a successful PSI and training.
  • Arrange all formalities to release the goods from port.
  • Arrange to install, test and trial run of the machine at customer premises.
  • Manage all formalities during successful handover of the items.
  • Conduct smooth post shipment inspection by the user.
  • Arrange Local training for user.
  • Collect acceptance certificate from end user.
  • Sales support within warranty period and then after for reputation and goodwill of company and principle.
  • Continuous liaison with customer and foreign supplier during the whole process of procurement and thereafter during warranty period.

Technical Support

To provide best service to our valued customers, our engineering section consists of skilled and professional engineers. They are capable to install, operate and maintenance of the supplied machine at our customer site.

Marketing & Customer Service

To provide best service to our valued clients, our marketing section consists of very skilled and well behaved professional marketers. Our customer service department is well aware to fulfill all sorts of requirements of our clients.
