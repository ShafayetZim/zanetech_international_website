Our Services

Main Services

Our main services are as follows-

Budget quotation, evaluation, vetting for standardization process, and follow up with purchase of tender documents/schedule, preparation of tender offers (technical and financial) with all certificates and other documentations and tender offer submission with bid bond.

  • Pursuing technical offer with procurement authority (technical evaluation committee) and users.
  • After technical acceptance follow up with procurement authority for opening financial offer.
  • Once become price lowest, pursuing procurement proposal and signing the contract between customer and foreign supplier.
  • Depositing of PG (Performance bond from foreign bank) send by foreign supplier.
  • Execution and following up opening LC by procurement authority and respective bank.
  • Coordinate for a successful PSI and training (if required)
  • Formalities in customs, custom duties, port charges etc. and collection of shipment from port, carrying and handover to customer premises (Supplier's responsibility according to contract).
  • Arrange to install, test and trial run of the machine at customer premises.
  • Manage consignee and user during successful handover of the items.
  • Conduct smooth post shipment inspection by the user.
  • Local training of the user/operator etc.
  • Collect acceptance certificate from end user and send to supplier/principle for release rest of the payment 20% LC and finally official process with customer for collect 10% PG.
  • After sales support for 01 year and then after for reputation and goodwill of company and principle.
  • Continuous liaison with customer and foreign supplier during the whole process of procurement and thereafter during warranty period.
